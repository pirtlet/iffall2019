By Stacy Title


This recipe has been crafted over generations in the Title-Penner tree. These chocolate chip cookies will delight whoever tries them--the perfect midnight treat or dinner dessert!

  • Prep: 15 minutes

  • Total: 1 hour 30 minutes

  • Serves: 1 dozen

Ingredients
  • 1 cup and 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 stick unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 1/4 granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup bittersweet chocolate chips
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 375â€™
  2. Cream the butter with the sugars and vanilla.
  3. Beat in the egg.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, soda and salt, and add to batter, beating.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Bake on ungreasedcookie sheets (Note: I use parchment paper) for approx. 8-10 minutes, until just golden.
